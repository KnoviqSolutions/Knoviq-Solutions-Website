KNOVIQ SOLUTIONS

We help growing businesses make smart decisions using data they already have

Businesses use Knoviq Solutions to help translate their data into meaningful stories to bring together their team to help solve some of their most challenging problems and establish actionability. Knoviq Solutions is focused on helping clients sift through all the chaotic and repetitive noise in their data, help them understand what is relevant and then make good use of that information to assess likely outcomes and also assist them in accelerating the pace of making informed decisions. We are a team of data enthusiasts who operate in our clients' best interest, according to our core values and professional ethics. We partner with you to help understand your goals and priorities.

Business intelligence has historically been reserved for large companies and corporations, leaving smaller to mid-size companies to piece together their reporting capabilities. At Knoviq Solutions, we focus on empowering our clients for growth to understand the power of their data and to use it to overcome their challenges. We help you to translate your analytics to english so that you and your team can reach your goals, well-informed and data driven. With Knoviq Solutions, we help growing businesses make smarter and better decisions using one of their greatest assets they already have: their data.

Data Strategy:

Before addressing your data needs, it is imperative to understand your goals and objectives. Subsequently, we focus on using data strategically to solve your business problems. Knoviq Solutions understands how your data strategy can help to improve your acquisition, storage, management, sharing, and use of data.

Data Management & Integration:

Most businesses have multiple data sources from various systems. This could result in mismanagement of your data. Knoviq Solutions imports and manages your data in one central location. Having your data managed and integrated centrally allows our dashboards to deliver consistent and reliable insights.

Reporting:

Our reporting is crafted to give you automated, custom, and timely analysis of your business's performance on demand.

Dashboards:

Our dashboards are powered by SQL, python and R to help automate your metrics. Our concise reporting is intended to keep you focused on the details that matter the most to your business. We've built them so that your team can interact and ask more meaningful questions to help solve your business problems.
